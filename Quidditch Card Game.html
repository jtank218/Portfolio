Quidditch Card Game

A place for you to get the info on the progress of our Quidditch Card Game!

First pass at alpha

Welcome one and all! We are happy to start this blog to keep you all up to date in one central location instead of spreading it out on facebook. This will be a place for us to talk about the game production process, especially the unique challenges of creating a card game. We are doing this as a fun experiment and hope that when the time comes everyone will be as excited to play the game as we are to be designing it.

Right now, as the title says, we are making our first pass at alpha. This means we have the first draft of the rules for playtesting complete, our first set of cards, and are going to be testing our mechanics. Most have probably seen the cards already (be it on facebook, QCB’s indiegogo, or as the cover photo here) and have some questions about them. We would like you to keep in mind that while there has been some thought about realism for the values on the cards, this is was secondary to wanting to work on the core mechanics of the game. We will be getting to more realistic values after we refine the rules a little bit more.

Thank you for joining us on this exciting ride!
